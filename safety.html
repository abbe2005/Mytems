HOW DO I AVOID GETTING SCAMMED?


You should be aware of the following tips to avoid getting scammed:

- You must take enough informations about the seller

- You must be aware of the seller's reputation

- You must be aware of the seller's reviews

- Ask about the seller's return policy

- Ask about the seller's warranty

- Do not give your credit card informations to the seller
